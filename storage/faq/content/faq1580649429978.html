The answer is maybe you can use SM connectors on MM but NOT the reverse.

SM connectors are made to tighter tolerances - as is SM fiber - so the ferrule hole may be too small for some MM fibers.

MM connectors have bigger holes for the fiber and will have high loss (>1dB) with SM.

Also MM connectors may not be PC (physical contact) polish - terrible for return loss.

MM fiber may not fit the smaller hole in SM connectors.
